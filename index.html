Project 2

HTML and CSS Project: Sales Website

Project 2 consists of creating a sales webpage. The purpose of project 2 is to apply the knowledge of colors, image manipulation, animation, slideshow, and modal window using CSS to create a simple sales page.

I created a website about HairCare Shampoo and Conditioner Distributor. On the webpage I presented different hair car products such as shampoos and conditioners. I created a slideshow advertising the many product sales and used hero images to present the hair care products. I presented three different shampoos and three different conditioners using modal windows.
